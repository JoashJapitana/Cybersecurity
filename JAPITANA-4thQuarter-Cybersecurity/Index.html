By: Joash Cafe Japitana

Cybersecurity is the protection of technological and inter-connected systems such as hardware/software, networks, programs, applications and most importantly, Data. It aims to provide security protection against cyber attacks and unauthorized individuals. The development of cybersecurity is crucial in the modern day's world because it safeguards our private data and personal information from individuals that may use your information for their own benefit.
